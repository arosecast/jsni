Members

Membership Term One year. Membership is for one year (September until June) and is renewed in September
Memberships Individuals : £15.00
Families : £25.00
Students : £10.00
Senior citizens : £10.00
How to Join Please download and complete the Application Form and bring it, along with your membership fee, to one of our meetings.
How to Pay You can pay in cash or cheque at any of our meetings. The cheque should be made payable to "Japan Society of Northern Ireland".
