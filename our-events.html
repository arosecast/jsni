Opening Party

Date  /  Sat 24th September 2022
Time  /  2-5pm
Location  / Merville House, Belfast

About  / 

The theme of the party will be "Bon Odori"( the Japanese traditional folk dance), and we would like to dance Bon Odori with you all. If you have a yukata( Japanese Summer Kimono), please feel free to wear it. We would also like to serve Yakisoba ( Fried noodles). Vegetarian version also available.

This will be our first face-to-face event in a while. Let's catch up with everyone and make new friends !!

We will ask you if you would like to become a one-year member at the opening event:
Family membership - £25
Individual membership - £15
Students/Seniors - £10
or entry fee of £5 per event (Children under 18 years old are free)
