Family Christmas Party

Date  /  3rd December, 2016
Time  /  2pm-5pm
Location  / Donaghadee Sailing Club (TBC)

About  / 
Our Christmas party is going to be held at Donaghdee Sailing Club once again, full of fun with Pot Luck, Santa's visit, kids entertainment, present exchange etc.

The cost of this event is FREE. But as this is a pot luck party, please bring one dish per adult.

Also, bring a little present for a grown-up, worth about £3, wrapped without name if you would like to take part in the gift exchange at the end of party.

If you are bringing a child/children to the party, please bring small present for them. SANTA will make a visit during the party, and give it to your child. Make sure the present is wrapped with your child's name clearly written in English. Please pass the present to committee member when you arrive.
